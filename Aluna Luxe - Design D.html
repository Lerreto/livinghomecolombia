Natural capital

Where wealth has wings.

Valle del Cauca ranks among the country's richest departments for birdlife, and aviturismo — birdwatching — is one of Colombia's fastest-growing forms of nature tourism. The finca does its part: its old trees, pastures and fruit groves are a daily refuge for tanagers, hummingbirds and migratory flocks — quiet environmental value that deepens every year.

The Town · Pueblo Mágico

Ginebra — the Valle's best-kept retirement secret.

Ginebra is an official Pueblo Mágico of Valle del Cauca, 40–52 km northeast of Cali — surrounded by colonial haciendas, vineyards of Isabella grapes, and ecosystems from tropical lowland to high-altitude páramo, all in one municipality. It is the cultural and musical reference of the Valle, with two world-class artisanal wine experiences within walking distance of each other.

21,000 locals. Zero mass tourism. 3,770 Google reviews. The town English and French speakers are already discovering — and coming back to.

01

24°C — every single day

No seasons. No heating bill. No winter coat. No grey Novembers, ever. Ecosystems from 1,000 to 4,000 m give it one of the most comfortable year-round climates in South America.

02

Colombia's only wine town

Two artisanal wine museums in the same town — Cava del Rancho (⭐4.8) and Vita Vid (⭐4.6). Grape-to-glass tastings of the Isabella variety. An experience that doesn't exist in Medellín or Cartagena. It exists here.

03

A festival running since 1975

The Festival Mono Núñez — National Cultural Heritage — is the most important Andean music festival in Latin America. 51 consecutive years, held a 15-minute walk from the property.

04

A free waterfall, 15 minutes away

Cascada Puente Piedra — ⭐4.7, 505 Google reviews. Natural, free, open 24 hours, and already drawing international visitors who review it in English.

05

City access without city noise

A city of 2.2 million — hospitals, universities, international dining and an international airport — sits under an hour away. You keep the peace of the countryside without giving up a thing.

Authentic, affordable and quietly growing — and not yet overrun.
